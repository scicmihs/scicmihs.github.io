Sponsorships

The Merritt Island High School Band program is hosting its 18th annual Space Coast Island Classic (SCIC) band competition on October 7, 2023. This is a Florida Marching Band Competition (FMBC) regional event that draws approximately 15-20 bands from all over Central Florida. Hosting this competition is one of the many ways that the Merritt Island High School band raises funds for operating expenses. We are reaching out to our community looking for a few businesses to help sponsor this event. The Merritt Island High School Band Booster Organization (MIHSBBO)is a registered 501©3 non-profit organization, so any donations are tax deductible!
