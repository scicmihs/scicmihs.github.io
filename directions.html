Directions to SCIC

From SR528 (The Beachline): Take Exit 49 and turn South on to SR3 (Courtenay Parkway). In 2 miles, arrive at MIHS on the left.  Equipment Trucks: Turn left at the light at Mustang Way and Courtenay Parkway.  Band buses:  Turn left at the light at Grove Blvd. and Courtenay Parkway and follow the road around and to the right onto the bus loop.

From SR520 (Merritt Island Causeway):Turn North on SR3 (Courtenay Parkway). In 1.4 miles, arrive at MIHS on the right. Equipment Trucks: Turn right at the light at Mustang Way and Courtenay Parkway.  Band buses: Turn right at the light at Grove Blvd. and Courtenay Parkway and follow the road around and to the right onto the bus loop.  

Spectator Parking: Turn left (coming from the 528) or right (coming from the 520) onto Grove Blvd. Make a right into the general lot for personal vehicle and spectator parking.

A student guide will meet the buses on the bus loop. A Band Booster volunteer will meet the equipment trucks at the front of the school.
